The basic package is suitable for property owners who wish to keep their property available for times throughout the year. The basic package is available at a minimal fee to cover management and maintenance services.

The basic package includes the following services:

 

 

We will take care of any problems which arise in relation to the property.

We will make regular inspections of the property.

We will ensure that utility payments are made out to the appropriate authorities in time.

We will collect and transfer the owner’s mail.

We will represent the owner’s interests to the building committee.

We will prepare the property before the owner or the owner use it.

Continuous care and maintenance – repairing defects and dealing with problems which arise, only using reliable professionals who we have checked to conduct repairs.

Transparency and reliability: we will send a quarterly report to the client with a summary of activities.

For clients interested in the basic package, we offer to add the short term service which allows rental of the property for short term periods when the property isn’t being used by its owners.

This package is suitable for owners interested in making income from their property whilst still being able to use it for short durations throughout the year.

The short term service is suitable for furnished apartments located in specific areas.

 

In addition to the services of the basic package, the short rental services include the following:

We can make recommendations, where required, about how to improve the property.

We will evaluate the maximum income that can be earned from the property based on its condition and the market.

We manage your search for tenants, including preparing and showing the property.

Strict and professional screening of potential renters.

Creating a rental agreement by the lawyer. Signing the contract will take place in the lawyer’s presence.

Preparing the property for the renter’s occupation as well as photographic documentation of its condition.

At the end of the rental period, an inspection will be carried out to ensure that the tenants have fulfilled their part of the agreement, including property payments and expenses.

The Inclusive package includes the following services:

 

 

Prior to rental

 

We will take care of any problems which arise in relation to the property.

We can make recommendations, where required, about how to improve the property.

We will evaluate the maximum income that can be earned from the property based on its condition, location and the market.

We manage your search for tenants, including preparing and showing the property.

We screen potential renters including an evaluation of their financial capabilities.

We will write the rental agreement, using a qualified and experienced lawyer.

We will prepare the property for the renter’s occupation and keep photographic documentation of its condition throughout the rental agreement.

Calibration of the utility payments (taxes, water and electricity).

We will hand over the key to tenant, giving them access to the property.

On Going Property Management

 

Evaluation and inspection – occasional inspection to ensure that the tenant is fulfilling his obligations.

Continuous care and maintenance – repairing defects and dealing with problems which arise, only using reliable professionals who we have checked to conduct repairs.

Transparency and reliability – we will send a quarterly report to the client with a summary of activities.

Legal services – the legal services we provide include creating a rental agreement and preparation of warning letters in the case of breach of contract.

Representation of client’s requests to the building committee.

Collection and transference of mail to the client.

At the end of the rental period, an inspection will be carried out to ensure that the tenants have fulfilled their part of the agreement, including property payments and expenses.
