Property Management and Maintenance in Israel

Home&Away specializes in management, maintenance and rental of private properties in Israel. Our services will increase your income while saving you time, trouble, transportation and expenses.

Home&Away will take responsibility for maintaining your property at minimal cost. We will only rent your property to tenants who have passed a meticulous screening process. We will maximize your rental income by assessing market conditions and property improvement consultation.

The management of your property will be the responsibility of an experienced lawyer with a master’s degree in law and a team of reliable professionals.

You are welcome to browse through our management packages.
